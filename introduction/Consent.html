Thank you for your interest in our study. Please read through this information carefully before deciding whether or not you wish to participate in the study. This experiment is part of a research project carried out at the University of Konstanz. The aim of this research is to study human decision-making in groups, and the individual and social factors affecting human cooperation and decision-making. The study will be performed as an online group game and is expected to take twenty minutes to compete. In each round of the game you will be asked to make a decision after giving some information about the game set-up and other participants' decisions. You will receive a payoff based on your decision. After the group game, you will be asked to complete a questionnaire about your strategic choices. Please note that answering the questions is optional. After completing the experiment and the questionnaire you will be given a completion code and will be redirected to the Prolific website. After submission of the completion code, which requires you to finish the experiment, you will receive a fixed compensation fee and a bonus based on your performance in the game. No known physical or mental harm may arise as a result of your participation in this experiment. Other than your Prolific ID, no personal data will be collected. All the data collected in the experiment will be confidential, meaning that the participant's data is collected on a separate server where only the experimenter has access to, and will be fully anonymized before processing for scientific purposes. The result of the study will be used in scientific publications and the anonymized data of the experiment may be stored in public repositories.


Participants' consent form


Participants' right

Participation in this study is voluntary and you can freely choose whether to participate or not. While you can freely withdraw from the experiment at any time without any prejudice, please note that you will be eligible for compensation and bonus payment only if you complete the study and submit the completion code provided at the end of the study. You will have the right to acquire more information about the study by contacting the experimenters, whom you may contact via Prolific website or by using the following email address: expkonstanz@gmail.com.

Confidentiality and data privacy

Other than you Prolific ID, no identifiable personal information will be collected in this experiment. We will use your Prolific ID to process your compensation and bonus payment. Furthermore, we may use your Prolific ID to link the data provided by you in this experiment to your general demographic and non-identifiable information (age, gender, nationality, education level) provided through Prolific. Your Prolific ID will be removed from the data at the earliest point in time and only the fully anonymized data will be subject to scientific analysis. Please note that once the data is anonymized, we will not be able to remove your data from our data set. The result of the analysis may appear in scientific publications and the fully anonymized data may be made available to researchers other than the experimenter group and outside of the University of Konstanz.
